LuminoKrom® – Innovative and Sustainable Road Marking Solutions

LuminoKrom®, for which we are the representative in Turkey, is the world’s first phosphorescent road marking paint that glows for up to 10 hours at night. Absorbing daylight and glowing without electricity or CO₂ emissions throughout the night, this innovative paint makes roads, bicycle lanes and other transit areas safer. With its ready-to-use, single-component and eco-friendly nature, LuminoKrom® is the ideal solution for sustainable mobility. For more information, please contact us.
